About Lotus Dental

Providing exceptional dental care with a gentle touch since 2010. Our mission is to help you achieve and maintain optimal oral health with personalized care and cutting-edge technology.

Our Story

Founded in 2019 by Dr. Siva Prathap, Lotus Dental has grown from a small practice to one of Hyderabad's most trusted dental care providers. Our commitment to excellence and patient comfort has remained unchanged throughout our journey.

We believe in combining traditional values of patient care with modern dental techniques and technology. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest dental technology to ensure precise diagnostics and comfortable treatments.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of serving thousands of patients, helping them achieve healthy, beautiful smiles that last a lifetime.
